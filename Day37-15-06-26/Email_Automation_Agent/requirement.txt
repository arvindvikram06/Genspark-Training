We are looking to build a new telehealth booking system for our clinic, 'CityHealth'. Right now, we manage everything on Google Calendar and it's a mess.

Patients should also be able to cancel up to 24 hours in advance.

Since we are dealing with patient medical data, security is huge—it must be fully compliant with healthcare privacy laws. It also needs to be really easy to use for older patients, so no complicated menus or tiny text. We are hoping to get a beta version live in about 3 months before the winter flu season hits.
